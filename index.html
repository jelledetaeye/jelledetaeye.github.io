I’m a Spanish/Belgian Software Engineer (Canary Islands is home). I currently live in San Francisco. During the weekdays I spent most of my time @Radius Intelligence where I brew coffee and tell computers what to do. I joined Radius’ Search Team after spending three years in Chicago.

I’m on a mission to try all the almond croissants in the city. To make up for all those croissants I try to stay healthy doing triathlons. I’m part of the Golden Gate Triathlon Club Board and preparing my second full Ironman this year. Check out some of my past race results :).

One of my passions in life is to build things. I’m always open to new adventures or challenges, from new side projects to a new endurance race. If you’re looking for someone to work on cool ideas, drop me a line!
